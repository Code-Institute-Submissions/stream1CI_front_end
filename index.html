    About Me

    My name is Flora Fodor and I was born in Hungary, 26th May 1997. I was sixteen when I started thinking about pursuing a model career, but I had no idea about the industry at all. So I found an agency, got my first test photos taken and that’s how the opportunities started opening up. At the age of 17 I joined Avantage Models and I am still working with this agency. I received a lot of support from Avantage and I learned the ins and outs of how to become a successful model.

    I have started learning about the fashion industry and the commitments that comes with modeling in the heart of Europe. What I like about my job is that I can visit fashion capitals like Antalya, Barcelona or Budapest where I can work with and learn from the best of the industry.

    What I`m being asked a lot is: "How’s a photo shoot looks like?" Well, a shooting can take up to 6 hours or more. It depends on the preparation work. The first step on a photo shoot is to get your make up and hair done by a professional make-up artists and hairdressers. Then the stylists help you to change into your clothes. And after that... it's only you, the photographer and the camera.
