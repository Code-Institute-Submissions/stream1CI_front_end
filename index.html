    About Me

    My name is Flora Fodor. I was born in Hungary, 26th,May,1997.At the moment i work with a model agency called Avantage Models. I was sixteen when I started thinking about pursuing a model career, but I had no idea about the industry at all. Then i found a agency so luckily I had my first test photos in my hands. With those photos i was able to find a new agency with much more possibilites.

    I started to learn more about the model industry in the heart of Europe,and also that what are the REAL commitments of being a model. With modeling i could visit different places like: Antalya, Barcelona or Budapest where i could work with, and learn from others.At the age of 17 I joined Avantage Models. They helped me a lot, and I learned a lot from them about the industry, and how to be a good model.

    What I`m being asked the most is: "How a photoshoot looks like?" A shooting can take up to 6 hours. It depends on the preparations(makeup, hair) When you are at a photoshoot, the first step is to get your make up, and hair done by a proffesional make-up man and hairdresser. Then the stylist helps you to change your clothes and after that it's only you, the photographer and the camera.
